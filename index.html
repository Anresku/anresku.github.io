Welcome to DeathEmpire

An ambitious MMORPG for Minecraft, blending ideas from Albion Online and Isekai anime, set in a medieval world with an Asian-inspired backdrop.

Key Features

  • Rich history and detailed government system.
  • Magic, levels, and player statistics.
  • Player-influenced market controlled by guilds and kingdoms.
  • Procedurally generated dungeons.
  • Four distinct kingdoms and a neutral "Dead Zone".
